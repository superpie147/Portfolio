USER MANUAL

User manual for me

Appliances come with manuals; people should too. There are conditions under which I do my best work, and conditions under which I break. If teammates know both from day one, nobody wastes anyone's time. This is the honest manual for the tool called me.

WORKS BEST WHEN

Conditions where I thrive

  • The goal and its purpose are shared — we may figure out the process together or it can be on me
  • There's someone to think out loud with. The more questions I get, the clearer I think
  • When I spot a gap nobody is covering, I'm allowed to just go fill it
  • We take time to align expectations at the very start
  • Feedback flows both ways: constructive feedback and the parts that require improvement
HANDLE WITH CARE

Conditions where I break

  • Being handed tasks without being told the purpose
  • I think fast, so I sometimes hand you the conclusion and skip the reasoning. Just ask "how did you get there?" — I'll gladly fill it in
  • My bar for finished work is high, so I double-check details before handing things over. If direction is all you need — not a finished product — tell me upfront and I'll skip the polish
HOW TO WORK WITH ME

Tips for working with me

  • Give feedback straight, ideally with data — I catch numbers and direct words far better than roundabout hints
  • Want to know the reasoning behind my call? Just ask. I think best when questioned
  • Attach the "why" when you hand me work, and I'll run further than you expect
